# sales-and-marketing-2-year-analysis
Built an end-to-end Sales &amp; Marketing Power BI dashboard analyzing enquiries, admissions, revenue, and conversion metrics to deliver actionable business insights.
**Sales & Marketing Power BI Dashboard
(2-Year Analysis)**
Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing Sales & Marketing performance over a 2-year period using Power BI.
The dashboard provides actionable insights into enquiries, admissions, revenue, conversion rate, payment status, services, demographics, and regional performance, enabling data-driven business decisions.

**Business Objectives**

Step-by-Step Process Used
1.	Data Collection
•	Imported raw sales & marketing data into Power BI Desktop
•	Data contained enquiry, admission, service, payment, revenue, gender, and district information
2.	Data Cleaning & Transformation (Power Query)
•	Removed duplicates & blank values
•	Standardized service names and categories
•	Corrected data types (date, numeric, categorical fields)
•	Created calculated columns where required
3.	Data Modeling
•	Built relationships between enquiry, admission, and revenue data
•	Ensured proper star-schema design for better performance
4.	DAX Measures Created
•	Total Enquiries
•	Total Admissions
•	Conversion Rate
•	Total Revenue
•	Pending Revenue
•	% Share by Service / District / Gender
5.	Dashboard Design
•	Used KPIs, bar charts, donut charts, and slicers
•	Applied clean layout with business-friendly color theme
•	Focused on decision-oriented visuals, not just charts
________________________________________
Key Insights & Conclusions
•	Strong Conversion Performance:
Conversion rate of 59.23% indicates effective marketing follow-ups.
•	Revenue Opportunity Exists:
Pending revenue (11.56M) highlights scope for improved payment follow-ups.
•	Service Performance Gap:
Certain services drive higher admissions and revenue, while others need targeted marketing.
•	Gender & Regional Insights:
Male and female enquiry patterns vary by district, helping optimize regional campaigns.
•	Payment Behaviour Impact:
Nearly half of admissions are without full payment, affecting cash flow.
________________________________________
Business Recommendations
•	Focus marketing spend on high-conversion services
•	Improve payment collection strategy to reduce pending revenue
•	Run region-specific campaigns based on enquiry concentration
•	Optimize admission funnel to convert remaining enquiries
